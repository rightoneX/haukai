About Us

Haukai Restaurant specialises in Māori kai and is based in the Bay of Islands.

It was started by a couple who are keen on creating a business that would appeal to locals and tourists visiting the area. The owners work in the business full-time.

The restaurant is only open in the evenings, from Wednesday to Sunday inclusive.

The dinner menu features Māori and Pākeha kai options and drinks. See our current menu.

We also want customers to experience Te Ao Māori with the use of karakia for blessing food and other Māori culture elements.
